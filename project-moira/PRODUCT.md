# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A mix of curious beginners and seasoned tarot enthusiasts — primarily a solo owner, with the ability to share readings with friends. Two contexts: desktop for deep, immersive spread readings; mobile for quick daily draws on the go. The job to be done is simple: get a meaningful, beautiful tarot reading without AI, without accounts, and without clutter.

## Product Purpose

Moira provides an immersive, elegant tarot reading experience using a traditional 78-card Rider-Waite-Smith deck. It offers daily single-card draws for quick moments of reflection, and 10 curated spreads — from a simple 3-card Past/Present/Future to the full Celtic Cross — for deeper sessions. All 156 interpretations (78 upright + 78 reversed) are hand-crafted, blending educational foundation with warm, poetic delivery. Readings are ephemeral by default, with optional PNG/PDF export to save moments that matter.

## Brand Personality

**Elegant / Mystical / Warm.** The voice is refined but approachable — like a wise friend who knows the cards deeply but never talks down to you. The experience should feel like sitting in a quiet, candlelit room with someone who genuinely wants to help you see clearly. Mystical without being occult; warm without being sentimental; elegant without being cold.

## Anti-references

- **No gothic or dark-occult aesthetic** — skulls, black candles, "dark magic" vibes, horror imagery
- **No playful or cartoonish new-age clichés** — pastel gradients, chakra symbols, crystal-shop aesthetics
- **No AI-generated content** — all interpretations are hand-crafted
- **No SaaS dashboard aesthetic** — this is not a productivity tool; no data tables, metrics, or admin panels
- **No generic tarot-app tropes** — purple-on-black defaults, Papyrus-like fonts, star-field clipart

## Design Principles

1. **The card is the hero.** UI recedes until needed. Every view leads with the card artwork; chrome, navigation, and controls step back.
2. **Depth without intimidation.** Beginners feel welcomed and guided. Enthusiasts find substance and nuance. Neither group feels talked down to or lost.
3. **Stillness over noise.** Every element earns its place. No decorative clutter, no gratuitous animations, no filler. The space between things matters as much as the things themselves.
4. **Warmth through craft.** Hand-written interpretations feel personal and considered, not generated. The human touch in the content mirrors the human care in the design.
5. **Moments, not metrics.** Readings are experiences to savor, not data to track. Export is offered as a keepsake, not a log. The app remembers nothing unless the user chooses to save.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA** contrast compliance across all text and interactive elements
- **Reduced motion** support — all animations have a `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` fallback (crossfade or instant)
- **Keyboard navigable** — all interactive elements reachable and operable via keyboard
- **Color is not the sole differentiator** — card suits and states use icons, labels, or patterns alongside color
- **Readable type scale** — body text ≥ 16px, comfortable line length (65–75ch), adequate line height
